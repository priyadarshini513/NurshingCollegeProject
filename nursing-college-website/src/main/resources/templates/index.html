Why Choose Jyoti Nursing College?

Jyoti Nursing College is located in Bhubaneswar, Odisha. Established to provide quality nursing education, our institution is affiliated with a reputed university and accredited by INC. We offer B.Sc, GNM, and M.Sc Nursing programs supported by highly qualified faculty and excellent infrastructure.

Our campus includes facilities such as a library, laboratories, hostel, computer center, canteen, and access to hospital training. We are committed to holistic nursing education, community engagement, and research excellence.

World Class Facilities

KINS was inspected by INC in the year 2005 and permitted annual intake of 30 students for GNM program. 1st batch GNM was admitted in the year 2008-2009 academic session. In 2010 GNM seat was enhanced from 30 to 50 students. Thus 50 students were admitted in the 2010-2011 session. Basic B.sc,4 years degree program of 4 years was started from 2010-2011 session after approval of INC with annual intake of 40 students. In 2012-13 session 2 year Post Graduate Nursing degree was started with four specialties from the academic session 2012. The B.Sc Nursing seat was enhanced from 40-60 annual intake.
