About Us

Home / About Us

Think Nursing Career Think Jyoti

We offer a wide range of high-quality teaching and extra-curricular activities.

Jyoti Institute of Nursing and Technology is a reputed institute under KIIT Deemed to be University, recognized by the Indian Nursing Council (INC) and Odisha State Nursing Council since 2008. The college is committed to delivering quality nursing education built on scientific principles and practical training. It focuses on developing clinical competence, critical thinking, and professional skills in students, preparing them to serve effectively across various healthcare settings. With experienced faculty and a student-centered approach, the institute provides opportunities for academic growth, research, and a successful nursing career.

Our Vision

Our vision is to heal and comfort the suffering humanity with compassion and respect with advanced body of scientific Nursing knowledge, innovative & technological approaches to transform evidence based Nursing practice locally, nationally and globally to be recognized as Nurse educator, Nurse practitioner and global leader.

Our Mission

  • Provide quality nursing education rooted in compassion and ethics.
  • Empower students with scientific knowledge and leadership skills.
  • Promote innovation and excellence in health service delivery.

Hospital

JINS is a one stop solution for Nursing Education in Bhubaneswar, providing patients access to impeccable expertise. Jyoti Institute of Nursing Sciences (JINS) is an integral part of the School of Medicine. Under KIIT University, this school is committed to delivering promotive, preventive, curative, and rehabilitative healthcare services, while nurturing excellence in nursing education and services.
