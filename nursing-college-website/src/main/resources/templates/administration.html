From the Desk of Our Top Management

We are committed to delivering high-quality nursing education with an emphasis on academic excellence, ethical values, and a caring attitude. Our leadership strives to empower students to become competent, compassionate nurses and healthcare professionals serving the community and nation.

Prof. (Dr.) Priyanka Priyadarshini Rout

Pro-Vice Chancellor

Prof. (Dr.) Priyanka Priyadarshini Rout completed her Ph.D. (Production Engg) from BIT Mesra, M.Tech. from IIT Varanasi, and B.E. from Pune University. Her research includes metal forming, cleaner manufacturing, and quality engineering. She has guided 5 Ph.D. scholars and published 3 books. A Fellow of the Institution of Engineers (India), she has over 100 academic publications.
